Sansevieria Trifasciata

a.k.a. the Snake Plant

Overview

The snake plant has succulent like adaptations that make them resiliant to different conditions, extremely adaptable, and a very low maintanance option for a house plant. They have robust, spikey architecture that can grow up to 12 feet tall while maintaining a rigid, veriticle structure. They uniquely produce oxygen at night - making bedrooms a good location - and are able to filter significant amounts of toxins from the air.

There are several plants that have been discovered to filter toxins from the air, resulting in higher air quility, stress relief, increased productivity, and a healthier environment overall. They are a good alternative to air purifyers that are both natural and asthetically pleasing!
